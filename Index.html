Empowering Change, Inspiring Hope
Support for Underprivileged Children
RongMohol is dedicated to nurturing the dreams of underprivileged children. Through educational programs, mentorship, and essential resources, we empower young minds to break barriers and build brighter futures.
Prevention of Child Marriage
We actively campaign against child marriage, raising awareness and providing support to vulnerable families. Our initiatives foster safe environments where children can thrive, free from early marriage pressures.
Social Assistance Programs
RongMohol delivers a range of social assistance programs, including healthcare, nutrition, and emergency relief. We strive to uplift communities, ensuring every individual receives the care and support they deserve.
Our Work
Education Initiatives
We organize workshops, scholarships, and after-school programs to ensure every child has access to quality education and a supportive learning environment.
Community Engagement
Our volunteers work closely with local communities, organizing events and outreach programs to foster unity, resilience, and positive change.
